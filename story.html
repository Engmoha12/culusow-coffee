Our Story

A journey through flavor, tradition, and the pursuit of the perfect cup.

The Beginning

Brewing with Purpose

Founded in 2024 in the heart of Mogadishu, Culusow Coffee was born out of a simple realization: coffee is more than a beverage—it's a ritual that connects people to the land and to each other.

Our founder, driven by a passion for the rich heritage of coffee cultivation, set out to create a sanctuary where the art of the brew could be celebrated in its purest form.

Our Ethics

Sourced with Integrity

We travel to the most remote coffee-growing regions in Ethiopia, Kenya, and Somalia to partner directly with small-scale farmers. By ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices, we honor the hands that nurture every bean.

Each harvest is hand-selected and roasted in small batches to preserve the delicate nuances that define its origin.
